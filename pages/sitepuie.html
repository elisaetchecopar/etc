About & Work Puie

Overview

Puie is an Argentine cheese brand specializing in cow's and goat's milk cheeses. It is a particularly meaningful project for me, as my business partner and I started the company when I was 22 years old. Since then, it has grown into an established brand with a strong presence in the local market.

Role & Responsibilities

As co-founder and designer, I was responsible for designing and developing the brand's website. My goal was to communicate Puie's identity and craftsmanship while keeping the experience focused on its primary purpose: showcasing products and supporting wholesale sales. I worked on the site's structure, visual design, content, photography direction, and front end implementation.

Challenges

One of the biggest challenges was working with limited resources. At the time, hiring a developer was not an option, so I designed and built the website myself using the tools and knowledge I had available. This required making thoughtful tradeoffs, prioritizing clarity, usability, and performance over complex interactions and animations while still creating an experience that felt aligned with the brand.
